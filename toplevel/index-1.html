  • How does BuckleUp work?

    'BuckleUp‘ provides users with the only online dating app with the ability to work in an offline environment. This app would be able to connect people with other travellers at the terminal, or heading to the same destination, giving them a chance to connect with other people, either for dating or even just to meet up socially while they’re both at their destination. 3 Our app allows you to date en-route - during the flight or at the airport - and date on-the-go while commuting. The user will either fill out their profile manually with their meeting/sexual preferences or they can match one of their social media accounts. From this point, the user can either scan their e-ticket/boarding pass or put in their travel details if they’re using the app in a travel scenario.

    The app also works if they’re looking to date on the go, as it has the built-in capability of scanning matches in close proximity, regardless of the setting. Once they have been matched with another user they can invite them to chat, without requiring an internet connection.

  • What sets us apart?

    We are building the only dating app for all settings - online or offline - including during your flight and on your commute, allowing you to chat with & date with your fellow passengers without needing an internet connection.

    Frequent flyers, either entrepreneurs or tourists, spend an increasing amount of time flying, either en-route to the airport, queuing at check-in, going throughsecurity, waiting at the terminal, queuing again before boarding the plane, sitting down for however long the flight takes (if it’s not delayed) and then doing thereverse of this process at the destination until they get to their hotel, hostel or residence.

    The main point here is the incredible amount of down-time they experience in settings where internet access is all but inexistent, creating a gap in the market for acommunication tool that works in an offline setting. In a time when doors are being closed due to ignorance and prejudice, BuckleUp will aim to connect peopleregardless of cultural background, ethnicity, gender or sexual orientation.
