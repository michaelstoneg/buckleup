  • How does BuckleUp work?

    • Whether you’re up in the air, underground or somewhere in between, we let you to chat with your fellow passengers without needing an internet connection.

    • You can either fill out your profile manually with your meeting/sexual preferences or you can match one of your social media accounts.

    • From this point, the user can either scan their e-ticket/boarding pass or put in their travel details if they’re using the app in a travel scenario.

    • The app also works if they’re looking to date on the go, as it has the built-in capability of scanning matches in close proximity, regardless of the setting.

    • Once they have been matched with another user they can invite them to chat, without requiring an internet connection. The rest is up to you.

  • What sets us apart?

    We are all about making connections.

    The truth is travelling, either by flying, taking a train or just your morning commute, can be an incredibly boring activity.

    The main point here is the amount of down-time they experience in settings where internet access is all but inexistent, creating a gap in the market for a communication tool that works in an offline setting.

    In a time when doors are being closed due to ignorance and prejudice, BuckleUp will aim to connect people regardless of cultural background, ethnicity, gender or sexual orientation.
